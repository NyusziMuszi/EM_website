Who are you?

Hi, I am Eszter, I am a designer and member of Animorph. In the context of my co-op, I specialise in UX and UI for extended reality, but my interest encompass a variety of subjects, such as print, typography, creative tech and spatial projects. I have some prior experience with coding, but only to an amateur level. You can find my CV here. A lot of my recent work is on our site. You can find this site on my gitHub repo here.

Why do you want to join FAC?

I keep hearing wonderful things about Founders and Coders (as you know Theo attended while working at Animorph), and I would like to be the part of this fantastic community. The new format of one day a week suits my needs perfectly, and conveniently I am just two floors away from the classroom. I find having structure and fellow classmates really helpful, and I think FAC will help me achieve my goals. I think I will also fit well into the ecosystem, as our values as a co-operative at Animorph are complementary to FAC's vision.

What could you bring to FAC?

I would be more than happy to share some UX and typography knowhow. I could also sow some fringe ideas around co-ops, webart, and obscure dutch digital design trends – as needed.
